Green Guide is a guide who is always ready to encourage people to keep the environment clean and protected. Currently, a large amount of inorganic waste that is difficult to decompose and is disposed of carelessly will damage the environment and have negative impacts, one of which is becoming a breeding ground for disease. Green Guide is here to encourage people to be aware and participate in environmental protection. This is crucial because the environment is where living things live.

Green Guide is someone who enjoys educating and raising awareness of the small daily impacts that, when collected, can add up to a large amount. The positive impact is a cleaner environment due to less waste.

Green Guide maintains and supports the community in living a greener life by reducing waste (such as implementing the 3Rs, or using cloth bags instead of plastic), saving energy (turning off lights when not in use), cleaning and protecting the environment, and educating about sustainable lifestyles.
