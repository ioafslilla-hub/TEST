Truth Talkers, heroes whose role is to disseminate factual information or act as environmental educators, help people understand the real problems on Earth.

The biggest problem today is that many people are not yet fully aware that the Earth is slowly becoming polluted, due to misinformation, people who don't realize how small things can cause damage.

Therefore, Truth Talkers are very ambitious to help convey the correct facts about the environment, educate the public, encourage people to care more. With a healthy and clean environment, life will be much better, and humans, animals, and plants will live happily and safely.
