The Earth is aging, the air becomes increasingly polluted due to increasing pollution, impacting human health and the environment. Fume Fighters play a role in addressing this problem.

This is not an uncommon phenomenon the impacts are very dangerous, including hazy skies that are no longer blue, causing shortness of breath, allergies, eye irritation, and even lung disease.

Fume Fighters strongly encourage people to reduce air pollution by reducing motor vehicle use, preventing the spread of polluted air by helping identify sources of pollution, maintaining human health, and promoting clean air. Air is crucial for the environment.
