Fashion Fixer, a hero who helps people reduce the negative impacts of the fashion industry, especially fast fashion, and helps them choose more environmentally friendly clothing.

Many problems in today's fashion are becoming increasingly prevalent: fast fashion, which produces too much fabric at too fast a pace, wastes thousands of liters of water for washing, wastes water, high carbon emissions, and the use of plastic in many clothing materials.

To prevent this from escalating, Fashion Fixer helps address these issues by reducing clothing waste, encouraging reuse and upcycling, combating fast fashion, choosing environmentally friendly materials, and educating people on proper clothing care.
