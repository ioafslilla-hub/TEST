The Earth we live on is getting hotter and experiencing increasingly chaotic weather conditions. Forests are slowly disappearing, water is dwindling, the oceans are less beautiful than before. Meet a hero named "Veggie Vindicator," whose job is to help the earth through healthier, greener, and more earth-friendly diets.

Many foods have a significant impact on the climate, such as those whose production processes produce high greenhouse gas emissions, require large amounts of water, land.

Veggie Vindicator is always working to address all of these issues. This includes encouraging people to eat more vegetables, fruits, and plant-based foods, reducing excessive meat consumption, reducing food waste, and inspiring people to try "plant-based days".
